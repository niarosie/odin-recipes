Fluffy Pancakes

Description

Here's a recipe for some simple but fluffy and tasty pancakes! I've only successfully made this recipe without fuckin the pancakes up once but good luck!!!!!

Ingredients

  • All Purpose Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Combine all dry ingedients in large bowl
  2. Make well in center and add wet ingredients, butter melted and cooled
  3. Whisk until mostly smooth
  4. Set batter aside and warm up pan on medium heat
  5. Grease pan with butter and pour 1/4 cup batter
  6. Spread gently into round shape and flip when underside is golden
  7. Cook until golden
  8. Repeat with remaining batter
  9. Serve with butter, fruit, syrup, idc just eat that shit!!!!!
