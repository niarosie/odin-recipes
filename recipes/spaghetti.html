Spaghetti

Description

Here's how I make my tried and true classic plain ol basic plate of tasty spaghetti with tomato and meat sauce!!

Ingredients

  • Dry Spaghetti Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Seasonings
  • Water
  • Ground Meat
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Heat up a large pan
  2. Add ground meat to pan and cook completely
  3. Drain grease if necessary
  4. Add garlic and cook a little
  5. Add seasonings
  6. Add tomato sauce and water
  7. Simmer until boiling and then add pasta
  8. Put on low heat and simmer for 20 min/until pasta is cooked
  9. Serve and eat that shit!!!!!!
