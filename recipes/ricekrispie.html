Rice Krispie Treats

Description

Here's a super easy recipe for Rice Kris-I mean Marshmallow Bars dont come for me keebler elf overlords

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Mini Marshmallows
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt
  • Crispy Rice Cereal Copyright Tee Em

Steps

  1. Butter and line baking dish with parchment paper
  2. Melt butter in large pot
  3. Add marshmallows and stir until melted
  4. Remove pot from heat and add vanilla and salt
  5. Add cereal and remaining marshmallows and fold together
  6. Transfer to baking dish and cover with parchment paper
  7. Let cool, then slice and eat that shit!!!!!
